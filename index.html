Adobo
What Cebuanos call Adobo is meat marinated in vinegar, salt and pepper. Note that there is no soy sauce. The meat is boiled in this marinade until it is tender or the marinade has been absorbed totally by the meat. Oil is added and the meat is deep fried on slow fire until caramelized.

Ingredients

  • 1kilo of pork(Adobo-Cut)
  • Vinegar
  • Crushed garlic
  • Black peppercorns

Instruction

Combine all ingredients in a pan, simmer until tender or the liquid will reduce to half.

Removed the pork and let it dry for a couple of minutes, then put cooking oil to the pan and slowy put the pork into the pan.

Wait until the pork will be golden brown. Served it with a mixed of soy sauce, vinegar, chili, and calamansi.
