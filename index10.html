Ginisang Batong
Cooking ginisang sitaw means to sautÃ© the string beans along with other tasty ingredients.Ginisang sitaw is a vegetable dish mainly composed of string beans. This is one of the most common Filipino vegetable dishes because it is easy to prepare and budget friendly, as well.

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch string beans; cut into pieces about 3 inches in length
  • 6 ounces ground pork
  • Crushed garlic
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil

Instruction

Heat oil in a pan

SautÃ© garlic and onion.

Add the ground pork. Cook until color turns medium brown (about 3 to 5 minutes)

Add the fish sauce and sprinkle the ground black pepper. Stir.

Put-in the string beans. Stir gently. Cover and cook in low to medium heat for 7 to 10 minutes.

Remove the cover and stir to distribute the ingredients.

Turn off heat. Transfer to a serving plate. ENJOY!
